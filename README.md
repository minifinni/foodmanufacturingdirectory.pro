## How to Choose a Manufacturer

### Step 1: Assess Your Needs
- [ ] What's your current batch size?
- [ ] What's your target monthly volume?
- [ ] Do you need recipe development help?
- [ ] What's your budget per unit?

### Step 2: Initial Contact Questions
1. What are your minimum order quantities (MOQs)?
2. What certifications do you hold? (SALSA, BRC)
3. Do you work with startups/small brands?
4. Can you work with my existing recipe?
5. What are your payment terms?
6. Do you offer storage/distribution?
7. What's your lead time?

### Step 3: Site Visit Checklist
- [ ] Facility cleanliness
- [ ] Food safety procedures
- [ ] Equipment suitable for your product
- [ ] Sample their existing products
- [ ] Meet the production team
- [ ] Review their client list/references

### Step 4: Trial Run
- [ ] Start with small test batch
- [ ] Check quality consistency
- [ ] Review packaging
- [ ] Conduct shelf-life testing
- [ ] Get feedback from trusted customers

## Certification Guide

### SALSA (Safe and Local Supplier Approval)
**Best For:** Small producers, startups, local distribution
**Cost:** ~£500-1,000/year
**Requirements:** Food safety management, traceability, hygiene
**Benefit:** Accepted by most local retailers and food service

### BRC (British Retail Consortium)
**Best For:** Large retailers, supermarkets, export
**Cost:** ~£3,000-10,000/year
**Requirements:** Comprehensive food safety, quality management
**Benefit:** Required by major supermarkets (Tesco, Sainsbury's, etc.)

### STS (Storage & Distribution)
**Best For:** If manufacturer stores/distributes for you
**Requirements:** Temperature control, traceability, logistics

## Cost Guide

### Co-Packing Costs (typical)
| Batch Size | Cost per Unit | Setup Fee |
|------------|---------------|-----------|
| 100-500 jars | £2-4/unit | £200-500 |
| 1,000-5,000 jars | £1-2/unit | £300-800 |
| 10,000+ jars | £0.50-1/unit | £500-1,500 |

### Additional Costs
- Recipe development: £500-2,000
- Packaging design: £300-1,000
- Lab testing (pH, shelf life): £200-500
- Certifications: £500-10,000/year
- Storage: £100-500/month
