How to Choose a Manufacturer

  1. Assess Your Needs: What's your current batch size? Target monthly volume? Budget per unit?
  2. Ask Key Questions: MOQs, certifications, startup experience, payment terms, lead times.
  3. Site Visit: Check facility cleanliness, food safety, equipment suitability.
  4. Trial Run: Start with small test batch. Check quality consistency and packaging.
