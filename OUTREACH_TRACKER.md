## How to Find Contact Details

### Method 1: Website Contact Forms
- Visit each company's website
- Look for "Contact Us", "Business Enquiries", "Trade" links
- Fill out contact forms with the email template

### Method 2: LinkedIn
- Search company name on LinkedIn
- Find "Employees" tab
- Look for: Business Development, Sales, Operations roles
- Send connection request with note

### Method 3: Google Business
- Google "[company name] phone number"
- Call and ask for business development email

### Method 4: Industry Directories
- Food and Drink Federation (fdf.org.uk)
- SALSA directory (salsafood.co.uk)
- Search member lists

## Questions to Ask When They Reply

1. Can you send me a price list/MOQ sheet?
2. What's your current capacity? Any waiting list?
3. Can I visit your facility for a tour?
4. Do you have references from small brand clients?
5. What's your payment structure? (deposit, net 30, etc.)
6. Do you help with recipe scaling from home to commercial?
7. What happens if a batch doesn't meet quality standards?
